    my skills

    I specialize in 3D modeling for organic and hard-surface assets, with skills in retopology, texturing, character design, and concept art. I'm also experienced in C# programming and game development in Unity and Unreal. My toolkit includes Maya, ZBrush, Substance Painter, Photoshop, and some experience with Marvelous Designer, Houdini, and MotionBuilder.

    Game Art & Design

    I have hands-on experience developing games in Unity with C#, including scripting gameplay systems like character controllers, enemy AI, inventory, and UI. I've also worked on rigging, animations, and cutscenes, giving me a strong grasp of Unity's workflow and the link between art and code. In Unreal Engine, my focus has been on environment setup—creating materials, using Niagara for VFX, applying post-processing, and setting up cameras for cinematics and scene composition.

    Hi, I'm Paula, a third-year Game Art and Design student at Technological University of the Shannon - Clonmel Campus. experiences. I grew up in Croatia and moved to Ireland when I was 18 to pursue my studies in game art and design.
